About FurryPal

The FurryPal Animal Rescue was founded in 2015 by a group of

dedicated animal lovers who saw a need for a local organization

that could provide care and support for homeless and neglected

pets. Our mission is simple: to save as many animals as possible

and find them loving, permanent homes.


Together, we are dedicated to ending animal homelessness and

promoting the human-animal bond.

How it works

Adopt a dog or cat who's right for you.

Once you find a pet, click adopt and fill the adoption
form, our team will approve your reuqest if you meet our criteria

We will contact you on your phone or mail.
