Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I choose these trainers?

We partner with the best trainers in the city to provide your furry pals with the most comfortable service. Moreover by choosing these vets you will be able to contribute 20% of your fee to help needy furry pals.

How do I connect to the trainer?

Choose the trainer that meets your preference then click on the get help button under the trainer's profile, this will lead you to a message box, fill in the required details and send the message, the trainer will review your case and connect to you as soon as possible.

How much do the trainer charge?

The fees can only be revealed by the trainer, and is in no way declared by Furry Pals Organization.

Do we have to go to the training center or they provide home visit

Furry Pals Organization does not deal with this, please clear this with the trainer themselve.
